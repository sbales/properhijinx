About Us

Proper Hijinx is a new independent theatre company in Dallas, founded by artistic director Stefany Cambra. Proper Hijinx is looking to stir up the best kind of trouble by producing intimate works focused on contemporary issues. It is our goal to produce unique works that will resonate with our audience, focusing on the art of storytelling.
