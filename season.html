TAB1
Produced in agreement with Dramatist Play Services

January 28th-30th,
February 6th @ 7:30
February 7th @ 2:00

Studio Theatre at the
Addison Theatre Center
15650 Addison Rd, TX

Down on his luck, Eldon Phelps thinks the arrival of television produce Gina Yaweth is a dream come true. A million dollars and a chance to be a star on television? How can you turn that down? But then comes the catch. He’ll be given fame and fortune, but only if he agrees to kill himself on live television. An audience vote will determine his demise- will a compassionate audience save him from an untimely death or will he meet his fate? Tune in to find out in The Dead Guy!
